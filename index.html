Built for fighters

Forseti is built for fighters.

AI assistants can help, but nothing is more powerful than human spirit and determination. This toolkit exists to help you stay organized, stay focused, and stay strong when the system tries to overwhelm you.

Wolves shouldn’t fall like sheep — and neither should you.

Forseti gives you structure, clarity, and tools, but you are responsible for your actions, your filings, and your decisions. This app is here to support your strength, not replace it.

What Forseti does

  • Quick Jump — Federal Rules of Civil Procedure with search and a fast PDF reader.
  • Motion Drafts — Pro se forms and generated PDFs you can print or save.
  • Guides — Plain-language walkthroughs for real-world filing steps.
  • State Rules — Links to official rules; keep copies offline.
  • Deadlines — Rule 6–aware tracker with reminders and calendar export.
  • Case Profile — Per-case folders and Brokkr-style file ingestion.
  • Document Scanner — On-device OCR into your drafts.
  • Glossary & notes — Terms, bookmarks, and annotations on the rules.

Privacy & trust

No accounts. No ads. No telemetry. Your case files, notes, and deadlines stay on your device — we don’t run servers that receive your data.
